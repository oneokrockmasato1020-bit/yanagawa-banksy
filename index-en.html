— YANAGAWA NEW MIX BAR

YANAGAWA BANKSY is a new-concept MIX BAR in Mitsuhashi-machi, Yanagawa, Fukuoka. Drop in casually with friendly cast and an all-you-can-drink set — Yanagawa's first laid-back yet upscale night spot. Just 20 seconds walk from Yanagawa Station, marked by warm pink neon.

Solo travelers, friends, dates, ladies' night, after-parties, business gatherings — all welcome. Men and women alike. Reasonable prices. Everyone gets to shine.

Yanagawa tourists welcome. Perfect for an after-canal-tour drink, after-unagi dinner, or a relaxed glass at your hotel/ryokan evening. 20 seconds from the station — you can't miss it.

— FAQ —

Frequently Asked Questions

Q Is it walkable from Yanagawa Station? +

Yes — only 20 seconds on foot from Yanagawa Station. Look for the pink neon sign.

Q Do you accept tourists or first-timers? +

Absolutely. Many of our guests are tourists or business travelers visiting Yanagawa for the canal tour or unagi cuisine. Solo travelers warmly welcomed.

Q Are women welcome? +

Of course. Ladies' set is ¥2,000 / 60min, more affordable than the men's price. Mixed groups and solo female guests are equally welcome.

Q Do you take reservations? +

Reservations via Instagram DM (@banksy.s2) are appreciated, especially for groups, celebrations, or champagne towers.

Q Payment methods? +

Cash and major credit cards. No card surcharge.
